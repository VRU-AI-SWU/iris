## Definition
The Thailand Professional Qualification Institute (TPQI) framework is Thailand's official system for defining Occupational Standards and certifying professional competencies. Established in 2011, it defines competency levels for professions in collaboration with industry stakeholders. It includes an ICT track (TPQI signed an MOU with ICDL in 2017 to adopt ICDL as the national ICT workplace standard). Standards are searchable via TPQI-Net.

## Relevance to Iris
TPQI is the closest thing Thailand has to a national skill framework, but it operates at the occupational/competency level, not the granular skill level that Iris needs. It does not provide a machine-readable skill ontology equivalent to ESCO. This confirms our emergent vocabulary approach: no gold-standard Thai skill taxonomy exists that we could directly use as an extraction target. TPQI could serve as a post-hoc validation layer — checking whether extracted skills map to recognised TPQI competency areas.
