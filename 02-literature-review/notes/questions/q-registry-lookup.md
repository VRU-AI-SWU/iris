## Question
Can Thai company registries (DBD, SET) provide reliable industry segment lookups for the web search agent tier of our enrichment pipeline?

## Why This Matters for Iris
Tier 2 of our segment enrichment pipeline looks up company profiles when a company name is known but its segment is not stated. The reliability and accessibility of Thai registries determines whether Tier 2 is a strong fallback or effectively skipped.

## Initial Hypothesis
DBD (Department of Business Development) and SET (Stock Exchange of Thailand) are the most structured Thai registries. DBD covers registered companies broadly; SET covers listed companies with standardised sector codes. Together they may cover the larger employers in job postings, but smaller or unlisted companies may fall through to Tier 3.
