## Question
Should course credit hours weight the skill contribution to the programme's skill distribution?

## Why This Matters for Iris
A 3-credit core course should arguably contribute more to a programme's skill profile than a 1-credit lab. Without credit weighting, all courses contribute equally, which may distort the distribution.

## Initial Hypothesis
Yes — weight by credit hours, with major-specific courses (หมวดวิชาเฉพาะ) weighted higher than general education. This decision has been provisionally made in the brainstorm but needs literature support or validation from domain expert review.
