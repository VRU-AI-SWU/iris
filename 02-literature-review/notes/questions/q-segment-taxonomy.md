## Question
What is the best practical industry segment taxonomy for Thai context — and how many segments?

## Why This Matters for Iris
The segment taxonomy determines how finely we can break down market demand. Too granular (e.g., full TSIC) produces sparse data per segment; too coarse loses actionable insight for institutions.

## Initial Hypothesis
A practical flat list of 10–15 segments meaningful in Thai academic context (Finance/Banking, Insurance, Manufacturing, Technology/Software, Healthcare, Retail/E-commerce, Government/Public Sector, Energy/Utilities, Telecommunications, Logistics/Supply Chain, Education, Professional Services). Map to TSIC optionally for formal reporting.

## Papers Addressing This
- [[tipsena-2025-predicting-thai-digital-workforce]] — uses 5-segment Thai digital industry taxonomy: hardware/smart devices, software/software services, digital services, digital content, telecommunications
- [[siddoo-2019-thai-digital-workforce-competency]] — 3-category EFA-derived taxonomy: Professional skills & IT knowledge, IT technical, IT management & support
- [[chaiaroon-2025-thai-digital-workforce-matching]] — defines 20 digital job categories for Thai market

## Current Working Answer
status: partial

**Most relevant Thai precedents:**
- **tipsena-2025** (5 segments): hardware & smart devices / software & software services / digital services / digital content / telecommunications — drawn from Thailand's official digital economy policy framework (DEPA). This is the strongest candidate for our digital sector scope.
- **siddoo-2019** (3 functional categories): Professional/Technical/Management — useful as a skill-level validation layer, not a segment taxonomy
- **chaiaroon-2025** (20 categories): more granular; category names not published in accessible detail

**Working recommendation**: Adopt or adapt the tipsena-2025 five-segment framework for the digital industry scope of Iris v1. This aligns with Thailand's official digital economy taxonomy, is empirically used in recent Thai research, and is coarse enough to have sufficient data per segment. Career paths (e.g., Data Scientist, Software Engineer) would sit within these segments rather than replacing them.

## Remaining Uncertainty
Full list of the 20 categories from chaiaroon-2025 (not fully accessible). Whether non-digital segments (Finance, Healthcare, etc.) are in scope for Iris v1. How to handle postings that span multiple segments.
